Mobile Development AccessU

Imagine an accessible map app...

...that locates accessible entrances.

Do you think we could make that?

AccessU is a project a group of friends and I started on in response to the OHI/O Fall 2018 Hackathon. We wanted to build an app to help students on campus locate ADA accessible entrances easier.

Both Flutter and native development seemed good... but React-Native was also a strong candidate!
We worked with all three before settling on React-Native.

Most of our time was spent learning how to manipulate React Component states to our advantage.

Our app uses Fetch to retrieve data stored in a Firebase Realtime Database. Learning NoSQL is challenging!

We plan to finish our map display and api and release them to the Android and iOS app store before August 2019.
